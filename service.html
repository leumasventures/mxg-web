What We Offer

Comprehensive Mental Health Services

Every service is built around your unique needs — with evidence-based care and a genuinely personal touch.

Mental Health Evaluation & Diagnosis

Thorough psychiatric assessments for ADHD, depression, anxiety disorders, bipolar disorder, OCD, insomnia, PTSD, and substance use disorders. We take time to understand your full story for the most accurate diagnosis.

Learn more

Medication Management

Ongoing monitoring and personalized adjustments to ensure safe, effective use of psychiatric medications — with regular follow-ups tailored to your progress and response.

Learn more

Therapeutic Support

Sessions grounded in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), solution-focused therapy, and client-centered approaches to build insight, develop coping skills, and overcome daily challenges.

Learn more

Substance Abuse Counseling

Compassionate, evidence-based counseling to support recovery, increase self-awareness, break unhealthy patterns, and achieve lasting stability and renewed wellbeing.

Learn more

TeleCare / Telehealth

Secure, HIPAA-compliant video appointments so you can access quality psychiatric care from anywhere — convenient scheduling designed to fit your life and schedule.

Learn more

Geriatric Psychiatry & Wellness

Specialized mental health care for older adults, including long-term management of cognitive conditions, mood disorders, and the unique psychiatric needs of aging patients.

How It Works

Getting started is simple. We'll guide you every step of the way.

1

Book an Appointment

Schedule online through our secure patient portal or call our office directly during office hours.

2

Initial Evaluation

Meet with our provider for a thorough psychiatric evaluation. Your history, concerns, and goals are fully heard.

3

Personalized Plan

Receive a treatment plan built specifically for you — collaboratively, so you're always a partner in your care.

4

Ongoing Support
