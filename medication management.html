What Is It?

What Is Psychiatric Medication Management?

Medication management in mental healthcare involves the careful, individualized administration and ongoing supervision of psychiatric medications to address mental health symptoms and improve overall wellbeing.

At Maxgio Healthcare LLC, our board-certified PMHNP conducts thorough assessments before prescribing, then maintains consistent follow-up to monitor effectiveness, adjust dosages, and address any side effects — so your medication always works for you.

  • Full psychiatric evaluation before any prescription
  • Individualized medication plan tailored to your needs
  • Electronic prescriptions sent directly to your pharmacy
  • Routine follow-ups to monitor response & side effects
  • Dosage adjustments based on your ongoing feedback
  • Coordination with therapists and primary care providers

Medication Categories

Types of Psychiatric Medications We Manage

We manage a full spectrum of psychiatric medications, always prescribed with precision and monitored with care.

😌Antidepressants
Antidepressants

SSRIs, SNRIs, TCAs, and MAOIs for depression, anxiety, OCD, and PTSD. Careful selection based on your symptom profile and medical history.

🧘Anxiolytics
Anti-Anxiety Medications

Buspirone, hydroxyzine, and carefully supervised benzodiazepines for generalized anxiety, panic disorder, and social anxiety.

⚡Mood Stabilizers
Mood Stabilizers

Lithium, valproate, lamotrigine, and atypical antipsychotics for bipolar disorder, mood dysregulation, and impulsivity.

🎯Stimulants
ADHD Medications

Stimulant and non-stimulant options for ADHD across all ages — including Adderall, Ritalin, Concerta, Strattera, and Wellbutrin.

🌙Sleep Aids
Sleep Medications

Short-term and long-term sleep support — trazodone, mirtazapine, and low-dose sedating medications tailored to your insomnia pattern.

🧩Antipsychotics
Antipsychotics

Atypical and typical antipsychotics for schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, psychosis, and as adjunct therapy for treatment-resistant conditions.

Our Approach

Safe, Thoughtful, Patient-First Prescribing

We never prescribe carelessly. Every medication decision at Maxgio Healthcare is grounded in evidence, individualized to you, and monitored continuously.

Evidence-Based Selection

Every medication we prescribe is chosen based on current clinical guidelines, your specific diagnosis, medical history, and individual risk factors — not habit or convenience.

Shared Decision-Making

You are always an active participant in your medication plan. We explain your options clearly, answer every question, and respect your preferences throughout the process.

Side Effect Monitoring

We proactively watch for and address side effects at every follow-up. If a medication isn't working or causes unwanted effects, we adapt your plan — quickly and safely.

Coordinated Care

We communicate with your primary care physician, therapists, and other specialists to ensure your psychiatric medications integrate safely with all other aspects of your health.
