Who We Are

A Practice Built on Purpose

Maxgio Healthcare LLC was founded in Richmond, Virginia with a clear purpose: to close the gap between the need for mental health care and the access to it. Too many people were navigating symptoms alone, receiving fragmented treatment, or feeling unseen in the healthcare system.

Our founders — Rose Idemili and Innocent Mario — each brought years of hands-on clinical experience across acute care, outpatient psychiatry, and community health settings. Together they created a practice that combines rigorous clinical standards with a deeply human approach to care.

Today, Maxgio Healthcare serves patients across Virginia, Washington State, and North Dakota, offering in-person visits at our North Chesterfield office and telehealth sessions for patients wherever they are.

01

Compassion First

We believe that genuine compassion is the foundation of effective care. Every patient walks in carrying something heavy — our job is to make that burden lighter, not to add to it.

02

Our Clinical Philosophy

How We Approach Your Care

We believe great psychiatric care is never one-size-fits-all. From the first appointment, we take the time to truly understand each patient — their history, their strengths, and their goals — before crafting any treatment plan.

Our approach is collaborative, transparent, and continuously evolving alongside you. We don't just prescribe and discharge — we partner with you every step of the way.

1
Listen & Understand

Every clinical relationship begins with deep listening. We take a thorough history of your symptoms, background, and goals — because context is everything in psychiatry.

2
Evaluate & Diagnose

Using evidence-based assessment tools and clinical expertise, we work toward an accurate diagnosis that reflects the full picture of your mental health — not just presenting symptoms.

3
Collaborate on Treatment

Treatment plans are created with you, not for you. We explain all options, discuss risks and benefits, and invite your input — ensuring every plan is one you feel confident in.

4
Monitor & Adapt

Mental health treatment is dynamic. We schedule regular follow-ups to assess progress, adjust medications, and refine your care plan as your needs evolve over time.
