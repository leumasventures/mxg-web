Rose Idemili

PMHNP-BC — Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner

Meet Rose Idemili, an accomplished Psychiatric-Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC) with an exceptional career spanning over 17 years in nursing and healthcare. Licensed to practice in Virginia, Washington State, and North Dakota, Rose has consistently demonstrated an unwavering commitment to providing compassionate, evidence-based mental health care.

With a solid foundation in nursing, she pursued advanced education and specialized training to become a board-certified Psychiatric-Mental Health Nurse Practitioner. This extensive experience equips her with a comprehensive understanding of psychiatric disorders, enabling accurate assessment, diagnosis, and treatment of individuals across the entire lifespan — from children and adolescents to geriatric patients.

Rose's clinical philosophy centers on actively listening, sharing the decision-making process of the treatment plan with each patient, and providing the ongoing support needed for meaningful improvement in mental health. She is dedicated to helping every client find balance, resilience, and hope.
