What Is It?

Psychiatric TeleCare — Real Care, No Commute

TeleCare (telehealth) allows you to receive the full range of Maxgio Healthcare psychiatric services via a secure, encrypted video platform — from your home, office, or any private location.

Whether you need an initial evaluation, ongoing therapy, medication management, or a follow-up, our licensed PMHNP delivers the same standard of personalized care as an in-person visit.

  • Secure HIPAA-compliant video platform — no app needed
  • Available across Virginia, Washington & North Dakota
  • Full psychiatric evaluation, therapy & medication management
  • Flexible morning, afternoon & evening time slots
  • Electronic prescriptions sent directly to your pharmacy
  • Ideal for patients with limited mobility or transportation

Be Prepared

What to Expect for Your TeleCare Visit

Everything you need to know to have a smooth, productive telehealth session.

Internet & Device

You'll need a stable internet connection and a device with a camera and microphone — a smartphone, tablet, or laptop all work perfectly.

Private Space

Find a quiet, private location where you feel comfortable speaking openly. Headphones can help with privacy if needed.

Have Ready

Your current medication list, any prior records or notes, and your insurance card handy for billing questions during or after the session.

Log In Early

Test your camera and microphone 5–10 minutes before your appointment. Click your session link and wait — your provider will join on time.

Technical Setup

What You Need to Get Started
